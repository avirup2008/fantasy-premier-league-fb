Putting a new spin to fantasy football

Hello and welcome to the Fantasy League competition, a Facebook based league and tournament modeled on fantasy football teams. Chances are, you already have heard of us. If you did not, scroll down to see what youre missing out on!

Dynamic

A series of tournaments and leagues to keep you and your fantasy team on its toes

Challenging

Mirroring the premier league fixtures, including all the ups and downs of double game weeks and unexpected blanks

Collaborative

Bringing together teams to fight as one for the glory of their club and lift the league title

Innovative

A one of a kind-format with the use of special rule and bonus chips, the next twist is right around the corner.





HOW TO PLAY — SIMPLE AS 1, 2, 3

1

Create your team at fantasy.premierleague.com and select your starting players and subs!

2

Find like minded people around you, form a group and become a part of your favourite club

3

Join the fantasy league tournament and lead your team to glory over the season
